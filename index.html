What Do I Bring???

  1. Level One License from Michigan Secretary of State, issued a minimum of 6 months before the date of the road test
  2. Segment Two Certificate
  3. Photo ID which can be a school, work, or expired ID
  4. Log of hours practiced – at least 50 hours total, including 10 hours at night
  5. Parent/guardian with a valid drivers license
